"A Home For Sydney" by Danielle Caro

Children's book and handmade dog treats

Sydney is a lovable Saluki who will win a place in your heart. A former racing dog from the Middle East, Sydney was chosen when just a pup to be a racer. She ran as fast as she could to please her owner, but when she became injured and unable to race anymore, Sydney was no longer wanted. She was alone and in urgent need of help. Sydney’s story is based on true events and is sure to warm the hearts of children and adults.
